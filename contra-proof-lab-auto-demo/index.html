What happens after you submit.

This is the canonical walkthrough for the mocked lead-capture workflow. It shows the operational idea without adding a real backend or sender.

  1. 1
    Visitor submits quote request

    The browser intercepts the form and shows a local confirmation instead of sending data.

  2. 2
    Lead is stored in CRM/sheet

    Production would record the request in an approved lead destination. This page only demonstrates the shape.

  3. 3
    Owner receives alert/summary

    The owner summary highlights vehicle, service category, contact path, and requested next step.

  4. 4
    Customer receives confirmation/follow-up

    A real setup could send a confirmation after review. This demo does not send anything.

  5. 5
    Human reviews before any customer-impacting automation

    A person checks the request before any message, booking step, or customer-impacting automation happens.
