What happens after you submit.

The demo shows the follow-up concept in one place, without creating a real backend, CRM, email sender, or customer commitment.

  1. 1
    Visitor submits quote request

    The browser intercepts the form and shows a local demo confirmation instead of sending data.

  2. 2
    Lead is stored in CRM/sheet

    Production would record the request in a real lead destination. This public demo only shows the idea.

  3. 3
    Owner receives alert/summary

    The sample owner panel summarizes vehicle, service, contact path, and the requested next step.

  4. 4
    Customer receives confirmation/follow-up

    A real implementation could send a confirmation after review. This demo does not send anything.

  5. 5
    Human reviews before customer-impacting automation

    The owner checks the summary before any message, booking step, or customer-impacting automation happens.
